# 🧠 Artificial Intelligence — Foundations for Building Real Systems

This repository captures my structured study of **Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning I** (Sapienza University of Rome), with a focus on developing **engineering-level understanding** of AI systems.

## 🚀 Philosophy
AI is not treated as a collection of tools, but as a discipline centered around:

> Designing rational agents that act to maximize expected utility under uncertainty.

## 📚 Covered Areas
- Rational agents and decision theory
- Search and planning algorithms
- Probabilistic reasoning
- Machine learning fundamentals
- Reinforcement learning

## 🧠 Approach
- Break down concepts to first principles
- Focus on intuition + formal understanding
- Highlight limitations of naive thinking (e.g., human-like AI assumptions)

## 🎯 Objective
To transition from:
- student-level understanding  
to  
- system-level thinking required for ML/AI engineering roles

## 🛠️ Contents
- Structured lecture notes
- Key definitions and mental models
- Exam-oriented insights
- Conceptual deep dives
